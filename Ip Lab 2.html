About Hamdard University

Hamdard University is a prestigious institution located in Karachi, Pakistan. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, fostering academic excellence and innovation.

Founded in 1991, Hamdard University is committed to empowering students through quality education, research, and community service.

Our Programs

  • Bachelor of Computer Science (BCS)
  • Associate Degree in Computer Information Systems (ADP-CIS)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Pharmacy (BPharm)
